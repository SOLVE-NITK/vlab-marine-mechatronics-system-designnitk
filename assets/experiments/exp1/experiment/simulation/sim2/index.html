Why Ballast?

When cargo is unloaded, the ship rides high. The propeller lifts partly out of the water and the bow slams in waves. Pumping seawater into ballast tanks re-submerges the hull to a safe draft.

Switch to Cutaway to watch the cyan water rise inside the double-bottom and side tanks.
